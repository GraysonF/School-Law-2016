IC 5-22-11 Chapter 11. Purchases From the Department of Correction 
IC 5-22-11-1 Requirement to purchase from the department of correction 
Sec. 1. Subject to section 2 of this chapter, a governmental body shall purchase from the department of correction supplies and services produced or manufactured by the department under IC 11-10-6 as listed in the department's printed catalog unless the supplies and services cannot be furnished in a timely manner. 
As added by P.L.49-1997, SEC.1. 

IC 5-22-11-2 Specifications for supplies and services 
Sec. 2. Supplies and services purchased under this chapter must: 
(1) 
meet the specifications and needs of the purchasing governmental body; and 

(2) 
be purchased at a fair market price. 


As added by P.L.49-1997, SEC.1. 

IC 5-22-11-3 Catalog 
Sec. 3. The department of correction shall furnish each governmental body a catalog with the following information: 
(1) 
Supplies and services available for sale. 

(2) 
Prices of supplies and services available for sale. 


As added by P.L.49-1997, SEC.1.
