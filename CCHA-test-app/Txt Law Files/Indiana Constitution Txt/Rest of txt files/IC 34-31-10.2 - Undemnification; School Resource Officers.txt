IC 34-31-10.2 Chapter 10.2. Indemnification: School Resource Officers 
IC 34-31-10.2-1 Definitions 
Sec. 1. The following definitions apply throughout this chapter: 
(1) 
"Public school" means: 

(A) 
a public school (as defined in IC 20-18-2-15); or 

(B) 
a charter school. 



(2) 
"School resource officer" has the meaning set forth in IC 20-26-18.2-1. 


As added by P.L.172-2013, SEC.9. 

IC 34-31-10.2-2 State to indemnify public school for loss resulting from the use of a weapon by a school resource officer 
Sec. 2. The state shall indemnify a public school against a loss resulting from any injury to a person caused by a school resource officer if the loss was the result of misfeasance, malfeasance, or nonfeasance in connection with the use of the officer's weapon. 
As added by P.L.172-2013, SEC.9.
