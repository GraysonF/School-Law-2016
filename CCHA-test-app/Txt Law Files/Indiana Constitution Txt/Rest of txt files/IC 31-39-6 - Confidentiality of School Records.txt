IC 31-39-6 Chapter 6. Confidentiality of School Records 
IC 31-39-6-1 Confidentiality of preliminary inquiry proceedings compiled by school officials 
Sec. 1. If: 
(1) 
a preliminary inquiry under IC 31-34-7 or IC 31-37-8 takes place in a school; and 

(2) 
the preliminary inquiry is conducted in the presence of 


school officials; any record of the proceeding compiled by school officials is confidential and is not open to public inspection under IC 5-14-3-3. 
As added by P.L.1-1997, SEC.22.
