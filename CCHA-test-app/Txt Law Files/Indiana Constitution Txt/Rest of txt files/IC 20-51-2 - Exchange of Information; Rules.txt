IC 20-51-2 Chapter 2. Exchange of Information; Rules 
IC 20-51-2-1 List of certified scholarship programs 
Sec. 1. The department shall maintain a publicly available list of the school scholarship programs certified by the department. The list must contain names, addresses, and any other information that the department determines is necessary for the public to determine which scholarship granting organizations conduct school scholarship programs. A current list must be posted on an Internet web site used by the department to provide information to the public. As added by P.L.182-2009(ss), SEC.364.
