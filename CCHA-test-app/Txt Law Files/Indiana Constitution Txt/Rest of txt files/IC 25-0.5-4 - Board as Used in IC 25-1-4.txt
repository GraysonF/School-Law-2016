IC 25-0.5-4 Chapter 4. "Board" as Used in IC 25-1-4 
IC 25-0.5-4-1 "Board" 
Sec. 1. As used in IC 25-1-4, "board" means any of the entities described in this chapter. 
As added by P.L.3-2014, SEC.5.
