IC 25-0.5-9 Chapter 9. "Board" as Used in IC 25-1-8 
IC 25-0.5-9-1 "Board" 
Sec. 1. As used in IC 25-1-8, "board" means any of the entities described in this chapter. 
As added by P.L.3-2014, SEC.5.
