IC 6-1.1-19-7 Tax levy not invalid because of failure of the department of local government finance to meet time limits; judicial review for school corporation of department's action 
Sec. 7. (a) A tax levy is not invalid because of the failure of the department of local government finance to complete its duties within the time or time limits provided by this chapter or any other law. 
(b) 
Subject to this chapter, the department of local government finance may make any order that is consistent with IC 6-1.1-17. 

(c) 
A school corporation may petition for judicial review of the final determination of the department of local government finance. The petition must be filed in the tax court not more than forty-five 

(45)
 days after the department enters its order.
