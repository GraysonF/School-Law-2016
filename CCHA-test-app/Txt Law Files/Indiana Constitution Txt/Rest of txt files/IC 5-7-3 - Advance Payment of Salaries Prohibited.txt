IC 5-7-3 Chapter 3. Advance Payment of Salaries Prohibited 
IC 5-7-3-1 Advance payments prohibited 
Sec. 1. (a) Public officers may not draw or receive their salaries in advance. 
(b) This section does not prohibit a payment under IC 36-4-8-9. 
(Formerly: Acts 1863, c.36, s.1.) As amended by P.L.37-1986, SEC.1. 
IC 5-7-3-2 Repealed 
(Repealed by Acts 1980, P.L.212, SEC.10(a).)
