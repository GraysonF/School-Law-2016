IC 5-22-6 Chapter 6. Purchase of Services 
IC 5-22-6-1 Procedures 
Sec. 1. The purchasing agency of a governmental body may purchase services using any procedure the governmental body or the purchasing agency of the governmental body considers appropriate. As added by P.L.49-1997, SEC.1. 

IC 5-22-6-2 Rules; policies 
Sec. 2. (a) A governmental body may adopt rules governing the purchase of services for the governmental body. 
(b) The purchasing agency of a governmental body may establish policies regarding the purchase of services for the governmental body. As added by P.L.49-1997, SEC.1.
