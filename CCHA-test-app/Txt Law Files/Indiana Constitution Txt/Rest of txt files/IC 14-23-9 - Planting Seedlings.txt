IC 14-23-9 Chapter 9. Planting Seedlings 
IC 14-23-9-1 Establishment of program 
Sec. 1. The department shall establish a program for the annual distribution and planting of seedlings by every third grade student in Indiana who attends a school that asks to participate in the program. As added by P.L.1-1995, SEC.16. 

IC 14-23-9-2 Guidelines 
Sec. 2. Seedlings planted under section 1 of this chapter shall be planted according to guidelines established by the department. As added by P.L.1-1995, SEC.16.
