IC 20-42.5-4 
Chapter 4. Emergency Measures to Maintain Instruction and Learning Programs 
IC 20-42.5-4-1 Duty to preserve instructional programs 
Sec. 1. A school corporation shall take the actions necessary and desirable to preserve and protect instructional programs, including class sizes, curriculum, or program offerings. As added by P.L.109-2010, SEC.5.
