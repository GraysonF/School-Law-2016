IC 21-43-1.5 Chapter 1.5. Priority Dual Credit Courses 
IC 21-43-1.5-1 Identification of courses 
Sec. 1. The commission may identify a set of concurrent enrollment college courses that: 
(1) are offered in the high school setting for postsecondary credit; and 
(2) receive state funding; as priority dual credit courses. As added by P.L.229-2011, SEC.245. Amended by P.L.125-2013, SEC.12. 
IC 21-43-1.5-2 Limitation on fees 
Sec. 2. The rate charged to a student for a priority dual credit course shall be set by the commission. As added by P.L.229-2011, SEC.245.
